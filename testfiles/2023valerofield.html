Tiebreaker

If your pool ends with a tie, this will help your pool admin decide who wins.

In 2022, for example, Scottie Scheffler won the Masters with a score of 10 under par.

You want to guess as close to the winning score as possible.

Team Name

Your Team Name is the nickname for your entry. This is what will show up on the pool leaderboard that everyone in the pool will see.

You can simply enter your real name, or some fun nickname for your team in the pool (like "Go Mama").

See the best golf pool team names if you need inspiration.

Name

Your Name is your real name, displayed so the pool admin and other poolies can identify you.

Enter your first name, your first and last name, or whatever you want to put so people know who you are.

Email

Your email address is needed so your pool admin can communicate with you and so you can login to update your picks if needed.

Your email will be shared with your pool admin and Easy Office Pools.
